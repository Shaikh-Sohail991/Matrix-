About ElectroTech Industries

Two decades of engineering excellence, innovation, and commitment to quality in electrical and electronic solutions.

Our Journey

A Legacy of Excellence

From our founding to becoming an industry leader, explore the milestones that define our journey.

1999

Foundation

ElectroTech Industries was founded with a vision to provide quality electrical equipment for educational institutions.

2004

Industrial Expansion

Expanded into industrial equipment manufacturing, establishing our first dedicated production facility.

2008

ISO Certification

Achieved ISO 9001:2008 certification, formalizing our commitment to quality management systems.

2012

Service Division Launch

Launched comprehensive service division for installation, calibration, and maintenance services.

2016

R&D Center

Established dedicated R&D center for product innovation and custom solution development.

2020

International Reach

Expanded operations to serve international markets with export-compliant products.

2024

Industry Recognition

Recognized as a leading manufacturer with 8 international certifications and 500+ satisfied clients.
